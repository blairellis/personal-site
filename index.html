I'm Blair. I lead the Experience Team at Maven where we're designing a new system to deliver quality healthcare to women and families. Before Maven I managed the product design team that owned the Etsy buyer experience.

I believe product development is a creative craft that requires collaboration, open critical hearts and minds, and hard skills. I love working with people that approach their work with big intentions. Some things I've learned along the way:

  • Proper research requires looking at a balance of trends in the broader world, the problems your customers have, and at the nuances in your execution.
  • Strategic planning within constraints is one of the hardest parts of building and shipping great, usable products.
  • Design is a tool not just for creating beauty and delight, but for fulfilling people's needs.
  • The power of storytelling and narrative in conveying the impact the products we build will have on people's lives.
  • Clear goals, an agenda, and ensuring everyone in the room can partipate is the key to not wasting people's time. Time is precious.
  • Active listening and trust is required to support people in their everyday work and longer term careers.
  • Good design is the act of bringing people with a variety of perspectives together to figure out how to make change.
  • Working people and the artifacts we leave behind are what make history.
